# App Specification: Gray House Graph
**Goal:** A high-fidelity, interactive relationship map for "The Gray House."

## Pages
1. **The Nexus (Home):** A full-screen 2D force-directed graph. 
   - Interactive: Dragging nodes, zooming.
   - Nodes: Labeled with Character Nicknames.
2. **Character Dossier (Side Panel):** Triggered by clicking a node.
   - Display: Persona, Group, and a list of their specific connections.
3. **The Chronology (Footer):** A toggle to filter the graph by "Book Part" (Beginners vs. Legends).
